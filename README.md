( on going )🔦 Beacon — The ultimate career hub for students. Expert-curated roadmaps for MERN, AI/ML, Web3, Data Science, Cybersecurity & more — built by top community members with ROI scores, daily opportunity alerts & Proof of Work profiles 

### Stop wasting years. Find your path. Trust the community. 

**Beacon** is a community-driven Career Roadmap & Resource Hub built for students who are tired of wasting years figuring out the right career path, missing golden opportunities, and drowning in scattered resources.

Roadmaps and resources are **curated by top community members** — developers, researchers, and industry professionals who've already walked the path. No random advice. Only battle-tested guidance.

## 🚀 What Beacon Does

- 🗺️ **Interactive Career Roadmaps** — Visual, tree-structured roadmaps curated by experts who've been there
- 👥 **Community-Curated Resources** — No fluff. Only resources recommended by top developers & researchers
- 📊 **ROI Scores** — Know the *real return* on your learning investment (Market Salary vs Resource Cost)
- 📰 **Daily Opportunity Alerts** — Auto-updated news on GSoC, internships, scholarships, hackathons & open-source programs
- 🏆 **Proof of Work Profiles** — Showcase your GitHub, LinkedIn, projects & certifications in one place
- 💎 **Premium Roadmaps** — Unlock in-depth, expert-curated paths with mentorship resources
